🔴 About Tuffy's Storm Prediction System
What Is This?
A proprietary severe weather intelligence engine generating a real-time Severe Weather Index (0–100) and occurrence likelihood for +3, +6, +9, +12, and +24 hour windows.
Data Sources
All data from the National Weather Service (NWS) API: hourly forecast (temp, wind, precip, dewpoint) and active alerts (watches, warnings, advisories).
6-Component Algorithm
Wind Speed (0–20), Gust Potential (0–18), Precipitation (0–14), Instability (0–16), Forecast Text Analysis (0–22, scans 30+ hazard keywords), Alert Severity (0–10).
Threat Levels
0–9 NONE
10–24 LOW
25–49 GUARDED
50–74 ELEVATED
75–89 HIGH
90–100 EXTREME
Auto-refreshes every 5 min. Not a replacement for official NWS warnings.

📊 About the Raleigh Wx Almanac
What Is This?
Year-to-date climate statistics for Raleigh-Durham, comparing current conditions against 30-year normals (1991–2020).
Data Source
Open-Meteo Historical Weather Archive (ERA5 reanalysis + NOAA). Reference: RDU Airport (35.78°N, 78.64°W).
Color Coding
Orange — Above normal
Blue — Below normal
Green — Near normal
