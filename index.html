About Tuffy's Storm Prediction System
What Is This?
A proprietary severe weather intelligence engine generating a Severe Weather Index (0-100) and occurrence likelihood for +3, +6, +9, +12, and +24 hour windows.
Data Sources
All data from the National Weather Service (NWS) API: hourly forecast and active alerts.
6-Component Algorithm
Wind (0-20), Gusts (0-18), Precip (0-14), Instability (0-16), Forecast Text (0-22), Alerts (0-10).
