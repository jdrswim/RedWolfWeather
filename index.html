About the Valvano Convective Threat Index
What Is This?
A proprietary multi-model convective intelligence engine blending 7 world-class NWP models (HRRR, GFS, ECMWF, ICON, UKMO, GEM, ARPEGE) into a unified Convective Threat Index (0-100).
Parameters
CAPE (0-40 pts): Convective Available Potential Energy
CIN (0-20 pts): Capping strength
Lifted Index (0-25 pts): Atmospheric stability
Agreement (0-15 pts): Multi-model convergence
